About Us

Discover the heart and mission of MFMCF OOU Ibogun, a fellowship committed to nurturing spiritual growth, fostering meaningful connections, and empowering students to lead with faith and purpose.

Here’s an updated and more engaging version of the text, starting with a brief introduction about MFM worldwide: Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM) is a global Christian organization founded on the principles of deep prayer, deliverance, and spiritual empowerment. With its headquarters in Lagos, Nigeria, MFM has a vision to propagate holiness and to bring people into a closer walk with God through fervent prayer and the power of the Word.

Mountain of Fire and Miracles Campus Fellowship (MFMCF) at Olabisi Onabanjo University, Ibogun Campus, is an extension of this global vision tailored to empower students spiritually, socially, and academically. Our fellowship is a hub for young, vibrant believers who are passionate about living out their faith in a dynamic and supportive community.

At MFMCF OOU Ibogun, we create an atmosphere where students can thrive spiritually and socially. Through weekly worship services filled with heartfelt praise and thought-provoking sermons, we inspire a deeper connection with God. Our interactive Bible study sessions and prayer meetings equip students with practical tools to overcome challenges and grow as individuals. We’re not just about gatherings; we’re about impact. From outreach initiatives to campus-wide events, we’re here to make faith relevant in today’s fast-paced world. Whether you're new to the faith or looking to strengthen your spiritual journey, MFMCF is the place where you can grow, connect, and lead with purpose.

Our Mission

We aim to be a beacon of hope and spiritual strength on campus by fostering an environment of prayer, worship, and service. Our mission is to mentor students into becoming dynamic leaders with strong spiritual foundations, ready to excel academically and thrive in life.

Our Vision

At Mountain of Fire and Miracles Campus Fellowship, our vision is to raise a generation of spiritually empowered students who will impact their campus and beyond with godly values.
