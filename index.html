About Us

Dr.DK Olokoya. The General Overseer MFM World Wide

Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM) is a global Christian organization founded on the principles of deep prayer, deliverance, and spiritual empowerment. With its headquarters in Lagos, Nigeria, MFM has a vision to propagate holiness and to bring people into a closer walk with God through fervent prayer and the power of the Word.

Mountain of Fire and Miracles Campus Fellowship (MFMCF) at Olabisi Onabanjo University, Ibogun Campus, is an extension of this global vision tailored to empower students spiritually, socially, and academically. Our fellowship is a hub for young, vibrant believers who are passionate about living out their faith in a dynamic and supportive community...
